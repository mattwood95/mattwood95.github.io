Don't worry, we're not here to judge, you not having a website is exactly why we're here, but if you don't already have a website (or if you're website is outdated!) it's likely you're either a new start-up or you've never seen the importance of it before. So lets take a moment to think about what a website is!

In it's essence, a website is a sales tool. It gives you an 'online presence' that no social media account can compare too (I'm not saying not to use Social Media, as they are great tools too!). It's one of the first places that new clients will go to in order to check your business out. If you have no website the clients may think you aren't a legitimate business and will likely fall in to the arms of one of your competitors. You'll be heavily reliant on leads from word-of-mouth and random walk-ins.
